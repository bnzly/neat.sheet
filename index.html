About & How To

neat.sheet is a tool for generating simple, yet informative, made-to-print Fantasy Football draft sheets tailored to your league's unique settings. Simply configure your options below and receive a downloadable PDF draft sheet to help you make the most of draft day.

Configure Settings:
Use the Configuration panel to customize all aspects of your fantasy football draft sheet. Adjust team count, draft type, roster positions, and scoring rules to match your exact league format.

Check Status:
The Status panel automatically updates to reflect sheet availability as you enter information. If available, download immediately. If not, you will be prompted to submit your configuration and a sheet will be generated within minutes.

Daily Updates:
Once submitted, your draft sheet will be regenerated daily with the latest player projections and injury statuses, ensuring current information through draft day.
